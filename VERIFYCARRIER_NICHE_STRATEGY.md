## The Carrier Pain Points We Solve

### Primary Pain: Broker Fraud

**1. Fake Brokers (Identity Fraud)**
- Criminals pose as legitimate brokers
- Carriers haul load, never get paid
- Broker MC number is stolen/inactive

**2. Payment Default**
- Broker goes bankrupt
- Broker refuses payment (claims damage/late delivery)
- "Factoring protection" fees eat into margins

**3. Double Brokering**
- Broker books load, re-brokers to another broker without disclosure
- Carrier stuck in middle when payment chain breaks
- Legal liability issues

**4. Load Board Scams**
- Fake postings on DAT, Truckstop.com
- Email/phone spoofing
- Bait-and-switch on rates

### Secondary Pain: Time Waste

**Manual Broker Checking is Time-Consuming**
- Check FMCSA broker authority
- Google search for reviews
- Check SAFER database
- Call references
- Check payment history sites
- **Total time**: 15-30 minutes per broker

**Our Solution**: 10-second instant verification with trust score
